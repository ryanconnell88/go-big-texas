If Texas were a Nation

it would be the 10th largest economy in the world

But our size isn't the only reason global companies-including 50 fortune 500 companies and 2.5 million small businesses -choose to go big in texas . The lone star state offers a skilled workforce of 13+ million people, a business-friendly tax climate and one of the nation's top deal-closing incentive programs.

Add that to our central north american location, world-class infrastructure and high quality of life and it's no wonder boeing, charles schwab, exxonmobil, hulu, kubota, sabic, toyota and many more recently chose to expand in texas.
